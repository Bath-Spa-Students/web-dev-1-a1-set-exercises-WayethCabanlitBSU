Author

Ryoko Kui

Delicious in Dungeon—or Dungeon Meshi—is a seinen* fantasy comedy manga written and illustrated by Ryoko Kui. It was first serialized in February 2014 by Enterbrain's magazine Harta, publishing a total of 97 chapters. The last chapter was published in September 2023
